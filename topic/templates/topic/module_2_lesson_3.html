How to buy bitcoins? It’s as simple as 1,2,3

1. Find a good Bitcoin Exchange

2. Trade your local currency (like USD or Euro) for Bitcoins

3. Move your Bitcoins into a secure wallet

  • Buy Bitcoins With Credit Card
  • Buy Bitcoins With Cash
  • Buy Bitcoins With Bank Wires

Credit cards is one of the easiest and quickest way to buy bitcoins online You’ll get Bitcoins instantly once initial verification of your identity is complete, it’s very convenient to buy. Typically cheaper fees than any Bitcoin ATM Machine. Cons: Usually you pay a little higher fees buying with a credit card, so if you're patient and not in a rush to buy then use a bank transfer to pay lower fees It’s not a private way to buy so your ID will be required to buy with a credit card

Cash deposit is often the easiest, fastest and most private ways to buy Bitcoins. You can usually receive your bitcoins within a couple hours. Can be more private since in some cases no personal information is required, it’s very fast cause no verification is required. It’s pretty much instant, there is no need to wait 2-5 days for a bank transfer to process. There are Bitcoin ATMs all around the world that let you purchase bitcoin with cash and you may have one near your home. Cons: Privacy comes at a cost; Cash exchanges often have price 5%-15% above market rate. Cons: Can be hard to buy large amount of Bitcoins Cons: Easier to get scammed so you need to be on alert who you are buying from and follow the exchange's rules.

Bank transfer is one of best ways to buy bitcoins in most countries. It’s a great way to buy large amount of Bitcoins. Using a bank transfer will usually result in the lowest fees, so you can get a great price on your purchase.
CONS: Usually requires ID verification, so not a good choice for privacy
CONS: It’s very slow; bank transfers can take UP to 5 days to complete (depending on the exchange and country)
